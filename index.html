How to Use This Choice Filling Order

This preference list sorts your eligible options in a sequence that maximizes your chances of securing the best possible college seat. During choice filling in the MP DTE portal, you should arrange your choices in this exact order:

  • 1. Dream Choices (Top): Ambitious options with borderline/yellow chances at elite colleges (e.g. SGSITS, IET DAVV). Placing them at the top is risk-free; if you don't get them, the counselor moves to your target options.
  • 2. Target Choices (Middle): Realistic options with high/green chances. These match your rank perfectly and form the core of your choice list.
  • 3. Safe Choices (Bottom): Extremely safe backup options with very high chances. These act as fallbacks to ensure you do not end up without a seat at the end of the round.
